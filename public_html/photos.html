Who are we?

Engineers! QueerSphere is the engineering LGBTQ+ group here at Skule. We want to get people involved in and aware of the LGBTQ+ community, to make engineering at U of T a more welcoming and inclusive place for all.

QueerSphere throughout the year runs socials and fun events such as Pride **** and Gingerbread Bridge Building, runs EDI training modules for F!rosh Week and EngSoc, and we're hoping to do even more this year!
