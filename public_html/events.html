Our Events!

One of the main pillars of our club is to provide fun and exciting events, general meetings for discussion and planning, build opportunities and more!

Throughout the year, some of our annual events and activities include: Pride Parties, Gingerbread Bridge Building, Pride Float Building and attendance groups, LGBTQ+ event groups, EDI training modules for F!rosh Week and EngSoc, and we're hoping to do even more in the future!

If you have any ideas for what you'd like to see QueerSphere do in the future, fill out this form!

To see our upcoming events, view or add our calendar to yours below! Event details and sign-up can be found in event descriptions. Keep up to date with all of our events ahead of time by joining our mailing list!

Have any questions about any events? Contact us through social media or our email!
